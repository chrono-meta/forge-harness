### Description
```
AI collaboration harnesses have become primary determinants of AI output quality. We present forge-harness, an open harness engineering toolkit addressing four failure modes: steel-quench (adversarial validation), source-grounding-audit (phantom claim detection), harvest-loop (session-to-harness self-evolution), and sim-conductor (pre-deployment transfer validation). Applied to forge-harness itself, the methods resolved 10 S/A-grade structural defects, detected 3 phantom claims, and confirmed multi-user autonomous deployment. forge-harness is available at https://github.com/chrono-code/forge-harness.
```
